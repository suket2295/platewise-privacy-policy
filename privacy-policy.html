Privacy Policy for PlateWise

Effective date: June 16, 2026

1. Overview

PlateWise is a calorie tracking and nutrition coaching Android app. This Privacy Policy explains how PlateWise handles your information when you use the app.

PlateWise is designed with a local-first approach. Your core tracking data is stored on your device unless you choose to use optional AI features or Health Connect sync.

2. Information We Handle

PlateWise may handle the following types of information:

  • Food photos and meal descriptions
  • Nutrition logs, calories, macros, and nutrient information
  • Weight entries and body fat entries
  • Onboarding profile information such as age or birthday, gender, height, activity level, goals, target weight, and nutrition targets
  • Coach chat messages and AI responses
  • Voice or audio input if you use speech-to-text features
  • User-provided AI API keys
  • Health Connect data, including weight, body fat, and nutrition data, if you grant permission
  • Notification preferences, app settings, and theme preferences

3. How Data Is Stored

PlateWise stores most app data locally on your device.

  • Food logs, weight history, body fat records, and coach chat history are stored in a local database on your device.
  • Food photos and chat images are stored in the app's private internal storage.
  • Profile settings and app preferences are stored locally using Android DataStore.
  • User-provided API keys are stored securely using Android EncryptedSharedPreferences backed by Android Keystore.
  • Temporary audio files may be stored in the app cache during speech-to-text processing.

4. AI Providers and Third-Party Services

PlateWise uses a Bring Your Own Key model for AI features. The app does not include production AI API keys. You may choose to enter your own API key for a supported AI provider.

When you use AI features, PlateWise may send relevant data to the AI provider you selected. This may include food photos, meal descriptions, coach messages, nutrition context, and profile context needed to generate a response.

Supported providers may include services such as Google Gemini, OpenAI, Anthropic, Groq, OpenRouter, Together AI, Hugging Face, Fireworks, DeepInfra, Mistral, or other supported providers depending on the app version and your selection.

If you use a remote speech-to-text provider, your audio may be sent to that provider for transcription. If you use an on-device speech option, audio processing may stay on the device depending on Android and provider support.

Data handled by third-party AI or speech providers is governed by the privacy policies and terms of those providers. Please review the provider's policy before using your API key with PlateWise.

5. Health Connect

PlateWise may integrate with Android Health Connect to read and write weight, body fat, and nutrition data.

PlateWise accesses Health Connect data only after you grant permission. You can manage or revoke Health Connect permissions at any time from Android Settings or the Health Connect app.

6. Camera, Microphone, and Notifications

PlateWise may request camera access so you can capture food photos or nutrition labels. PlateWise may request microphone access if you use voice input features. PlateWise may request notification permission to show reminders or app notifications.

These permissions are used only for user-facing app features.

7. AI Safety and Accuracy

AI-generated responses in PlateWise may be incorrect, incomplete, or inappropriate. AI responses are for informational purposes only and are not medical advice.

Always consult a qualified healthcare professional before making medical, nutritional, fitness, or diet decisions.

PlateWise provides an in-app option to report unsafe, offensive, misleading, or medically inappropriate AI responses from the Coach screen.

8. Data Sharing

PlateWise does not sell your personal data.

PlateWise may share data with third-party AI or speech providers only when you use optional AI or speech features and only for the purpose of providing those features.

PlateWise may read or write Health Connect data only with your permission.

9. Data Deletion and Control

You can delete individual entries inside the app where deletion options are available.

You can delete local app data using the app's reset or delete options where available, or by clearing app storage from Android Settings.

You can remove API keys from the app settings if supported. You can revoke Health Connect permissions from Android Settings or the Health Connect app.

10. Children's Privacy

PlateWise is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

11. Security

PlateWise uses Android platform storage and security features to help protect local data. User-provided API keys are stored using Android EncryptedSharedPreferences backed by Android Keystore.

No method of storage or transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page with a new effective date.
